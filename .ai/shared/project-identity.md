# Project Identity

NOVUS OS is an AI Life, Business and Financial Operating System.

## What NOVUS Is

- A decision-support operating system across life, business and financial workflows.
- A long-lived product that requires strong repository discipline before feature velocity.
- A system that should be understandable by engineers and AI agents within a short onboarding window.

## What NOVUS Is Not

- Not a chatbot.
- Not only a finance application.
- Not a repository for experiments that bypass architecture review.

## Engineering Implication

Every technical choice should improve maintainability, scalability, security, performance, developer experience or clarity. Product strategy decisions belong to the designated product strategy owner, not to repository maintenance work.

## AI-First Implication

Every technical choice should also make the repository easier for future AI agents to inspect, reason about and modify safely without relying on hidden conversation context.
