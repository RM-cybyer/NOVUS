# Voice Experience

## Purpose

Define the voice and tone expectations for NOVUS user-facing product experiences.

## Responsibilities

- Keep language clear, direct and decision-oriented.
- Explain trade-offs without sounding restrictive.
- Avoid chatbot-like filler.
- Support confidence without overclaiming.

## Voice Principles

- Precise.
- Calm.
- Useful.
- Transparent about uncertainty.
- Respectful of user agency.
- Focused on next decisions.

## Product Copy Rules

- Prefer specific labels over vague encouragement.
- Distinguish facts, assumptions and recommendations.
- Do not claim actions were completed unless NOVUS executed them.
- Do not hide risk in soft language.
- Keep financial and business guidance conservative and explicit.

## AI Ownership

- Owning AI agent: Fable.
- Collaborating AI agents: Claude for product voice, QA for clarity validation.
