# Dashboard Design

## Purpose

Define design guidance for future NOVUS dashboard surfaces.

## Responsibilities

- Present life, business and financial context in a scannable operating view.
- Surface priorities, risks, opportunities and progress.
- Avoid decorative dashboards that do not support decisions.

## Dashboard Rules

- Prioritize current state, important changes and next decisions.
- Group related context by decision area.
- Show freshness and source of data where relevant.
- Provide drill-down paths without crowding the first view.
- Avoid vanity metrics unless they support action.

## Expected States

- Loading.
- Empty.
- Partial data.
- Normal.
- Needs attention.
- Error.
